Effects, penalties & rewards (the primitives)

REQ-WDS-589141516172021

What

Every rule that moves a card, deals Progress, or changes HP, built as small shared primitives plus dispatchers that apply a player-card Effect, a Hazard Penalty (on discard), and a Hazard Reward (on resolve). Each primitive is (state, …) → { state, events } and assumes a legal call (legality lives in Tasks 5/6).

  • dealProgress(state, hazardId, base, bonus?) — amount = base + (bonus.amount if hazard has bonus.tag), added to progress[hazardId]; emit ProgressDealt{hazardId, amount, hazardTurnTotal}; then auto-resolve in the same call: if progress[hazardId] ≥ cost, grant the reward, remove from hand, emit HazardResolved{id} (excess wasted).
  • gainCard(state, template, dest) — mint a fresh instance to player-discard (default), player-draw top, or world-draw top; emit CardGained{id, dest}. (Shared by the effect's AddCard/AddWorldCardToTop and penalty/reward gains.)
  • returnToTopThree(state, ids) — remove named world cards from hand, insert into the top three positions of worldDraw ordered by the seeded PRNG; emit WorldCardsReturned{ids}.
  • destroyInHand(state, id?) — remove from the game (no zone); emit CardDestroyed{id}.
  • damage(state, n) / heal(state, n) — adjust HP, emit DamageDealt/HpChanged; damage sets status='lost' + WorldLost at HP≤0.
  • grantReward(state, reward) — dispatch the reward vocabulary; SurviveWorld sets status='won' + WorldWon.
  • applyPenalty(state, penalty) — Damage, SkipDrawNextTurn (idempotent boolean, no stacking), GainCard, AddWorldCardToTop, None.
  • applyEffect(state, effect, action) — the player-effect dispatcher: Modal (pick action.choice branch), Sequence (apply steps in order threading state; Barricade deals before returning, so a Hazard resolved in step 1 is absent from the return step), ReturnWorldCards/DestroyCardInHand (use action.returnIds/destroyId), DiscardThenDraw (move action.discardId to player-discard, then drawPlayer).

Validation

  • Keyword math: Baseball Bat at a Zombie deals 2+3=5 and resolves the 1-cost Hazard; Explore at a non-Hidden Hazard deals only 1; auto-resolve fires the instant turn-Progress ≥ cost, excess wasted.
  • Economy: a GainCard reward lands in player-discard; AddPlayerCardToTop/AddWorldCardToTop hit the right pile tops; returns land in the top 3 of the world deck; Regroup removes a hand card from the game entirely.
  • Penalties: Damage subtracts HP (and can trigger loss); SkipDrawNextTurn is idempotent; GainCard/AddWorldCardToTop move cards; None is inert.
  • Win/loss wiring: granting SurviveWorld sets won; HP≤0 sets lost.

Why

REQ-WDS-5 (keyword bonuses), 8 (DealProgress at a target), 9 (auto-resolve at cost, excess wasted), 14 (gains default to player discard, destination overrides), 15 (returns to top 3), 16 (Regroup permanent destroy), 17 (penalties resolve against HP/draw/decks), 20/21 (Door reward wins; HP≤0 loses). Sharing one set of primitives across effects and penalties/rewards keeps "add a card" and "deal progress" defined once. See plan Step 4.
