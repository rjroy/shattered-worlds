The reducer, dispatch & golden replays

REQ-WDS-12891011121320212228

What

Wire the action handlers into the pure reduce(state, action) → { state, events }, enforce legality through availableActions (no second copy of the rules), and prove the loop deterministic with golden win/loss replays.

  • Guard: if status !== 'playing', any action throws IllegalActionError.
  • PlayCard — validate the card is currently playable and the target/choice/returnIds/destroyId/discardId is legal using availableActions; else throw. Remove the card from hand, emit CardPlayed, run applyEffect (auto-resolve happens inside dealProgress), then check win/loss.
  • DiscardHazard — must name a discardable world card in hand (the Door throws); apply its penalty, remove from hand, emit HazardDiscarded + penalty event; check loss.
  • EndTurn — strict order: (1) move every player card in hand to player-discard (world cards stay), emit CardsDiscarded; (2) reset progress to {}; (3) consume skipDrawNext; (4) refillHand. Emit TurnEnded, CardsDiscarded, optional DeckShuffled/ActAdvanced, CardsDrawn in order.
  • createGame(seed): GameCore built on createWorld; the façade holds the snapshot, exposes dispatch and read access to availableActions/intensity.
  • Re-export the new surface through contract.ts and index.ts.

Id-accounting helper: assert every id is in exactly one place (a zone or removed) and every removal/creation has an explaining event. Golden seeds are searched once (e.g. run the sim until a win and a loss are found), then frozen as constants with their recorded action logs.

Validation

  • No carry-over: partially pay a Hazard, end the turn, its turn-Progress is 0.
  • Hold vs discard: after EndTurn, held world cards remain in hand and unplayed player cards are in player-discard.
  • Error contract: DealProgress with no Hazard throws; out-of-range Modal choice throws; Panic with no world card throws; DiscardHazard on the Door throws; returning a player-card id throws; any action after won/lost throws.
  • Golden win: a fixed seed + recorded log reaches won by forcing and paying a Door (discard Walker → Door penalty → pay 2) without ever resolving The Walker, byte-identical on re-run.
  • Golden loss: a second fixed seed + log reaches lost by lethal damage.
  • Replay equivalence: same seed + actions ⇒ deep-equal final state and event log; a different seed ⇒ different Act 1 order. Id-accounting holds across the sequence.
  • Tests run headless with no Phaser/DOM/canvas in scope.
⚠ Review gate (highest blast radius): a fresh-context sub-agent reviews the core before Tasks 7–9 begin — no hidden mutation, no rules duplicated outside availableActions, no determinism hole.
