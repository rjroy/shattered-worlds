What

  • New src/game/piles.ts: render the player draw pile and world draw pile as small stacks of face-down cardback.webp beside their existing HUD counts.

  • Both piles show face-down backs from cardback.webp, never recolored by the theme.
  • Stacks visibly shrink/grow with pile sizes as cards are drawn and reshuffled; an empty pile shows no stack.
  • The pile containers survive the per-dispatch drawAll card-destroy cycle (they are not recreated each repaint, no flicker).
  • Card fronts (S6) stay themed; the back is the only constant.

Why

REQ-VIS-5 makes the back constant canon the player sees; with no current render site, the draw-pile stacks are its home (decision confirmed with user). See plan Step 7.
