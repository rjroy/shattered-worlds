Loud, unmistakable hover-target highlight

pending REQ-FEEDBACK-1 REQ-FEEDBACK-4 REQ-FEEDBACK-12 (intensity half)

Dependencies: S3 (persistent containers + hovered-id tracking), S2 (targetGlow). Coexists with S7's hover hooks.

What

Make the hovered legal target the loudest read on the board, distinct on its own — beyond the current 3px border tint. Add at least one of: scale/lift, glow, or an on-card reticle.

  • On pointerover of a legal target (any targeting phase), apply emphasis: scale the persistent container up slightly and/or add a targetGlow (S2) ring/tint; optionally an on-card reticle. On pointerout, restore base transform.
  • Must be distinct from the legal-but-unhovered target border state (applyHighlight 329-333) and from the dimmed non-target state (dimAlpha 0.35).
  • Hover-out / state-change contract (owns the S3↔S9 seam): (a) pointerout clears the stored hovered id and restores base transform; (b) if drawAll fires mid-hover and the hovered id is no longer in legalTargetIds, S9's update path clears the stored hovered id and restores base transform; (c) if the hovered card left the hand, S3's reconcile destroys it (emphasis gone automatically).
  • Intensity scaling (completes FEEDBACK-12): scale the emphasis alpha/size by this.game_.intensity(). This is the intensity-scaled-emphasis half of FEEDBACK-12; together with S2's color seam it covers FEEDBACK-12 fully.

Validation

  • FEEDBACK-1 (manual): hover a legal hazard while a card is selected → the hovered target is obviously the loudest card (scale/lift/glow/reticle), distinct from other legal targets and from dimmed cards, with no color decoding.
  • FEEDBACK-4 (manual): the emphasis appears on hover, before any click.
  • Manual (seam check): emphasis is correctly cleared on hover-out and when a mid-hover repaint makes the card no longer legal — no stuck scaled/glowing card.
  • FEEDBACK-12 (manual): emphasis intensity tracks this.game_.intensity() (verified together with the theme-color check in S12).
  • bun test green; tsc/eslint clean.

Why

Implements REQ-FEEDBACK-1 (loud target read on its own), REQ-FEEDBACK-4 (on hover before click), and the intensity-scaled half of REQ-FEEDBACK-12. Per plan step S9.
